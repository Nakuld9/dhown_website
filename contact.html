Quick Answers

Common Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

We work with buyers of all sizes. Contact us with your requirement and we'll advise on the minimum based on your fabric type and specifications.

Do you provide fabric samples?

Yes. Sample cuts are available for all fabric types before committing to a bulk order. Share your spec and we'll arrange a sample dispatch.

What is your production lead time?

Typical lead time is 7–15 days depending on quantity, yarn availability, and current production schedule. We'll confirm at the time of order.

Do you ship outside Ludhiana?

Yes, we supply across India and have experience dispatching for export orders. Transport costs are calculated based on destination.
