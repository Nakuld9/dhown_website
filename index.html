About Us

Ludhiana's Trusted Knitted Fabric Manufacturer

DHOWN Hosiery Mills is a dedicated knitting manufacturer based in Ludhiana — India's textile capital. We operate a modern facility running 24/7 across two production shifts, supplying high-quality knitted fabrics to garment makers, brands, and exporters.

Our state-of-the-art circular knitting machines produce consistent, premium-grade fabric in a range of constructions, gauges, and yarn compositions — tailored to your exact specifications.
