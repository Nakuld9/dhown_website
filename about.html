About DHOWN Hosiery Mills

A dedicated knitting manufacturer from the heart of India's textile capital — Ludhiana, Punjab.

Who We Are

Manufacturing Excellence from Ludhiana

DHOWN Hosiery Mills is a professional circular knitting manufacturer operating out of Ludhiana, Punjab — a city that has defined India's textile industry for generations. We produce high-quality knitted fabrics for garment manufacturers, brands, and exporters across India and beyond.

Our facility runs on modern circular knitting machines across two production shifts every day — ensuring continuous output, consistent quality, and reliable delivery for our partners.

We are not traders. We are manufacturers. Every roll of fabric that leaves DHOWN is produced in-house on our own machines, inspected by our team, and packed to your specification. This gives us complete control over quality, lead times, and pricing — benefits that pass directly to you.
